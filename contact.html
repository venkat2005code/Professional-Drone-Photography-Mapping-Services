Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the weather is bad on flight day?
Safety is our top priority. If wind exceeds UAV thresholds or precipitation occurs, we reschedule at no extra cost to you. We continuously monitor live aviation weather.
Do you handle FAA airspace authorizations (LAANC)?
Yes. Our Part 107 certified pilots and operations team handle all necessary waivers and airspace authorizations before flying near controlled airspace.
How are large datasets delivered?
All orthomosaics, point clouds, and raw media are securely uploaded to our proprietary SaaS client dashboard, allowing you to view and download files directly.

Emergency Deployment Required?

For natural disaster response, structural failure assessments, or search and rescue support, we offer 12-hour rapid deployment.
