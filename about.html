Redefining the Sky

Aero was founded on a simple principle: To deliver the most accurate aerial data and breathtaking cinematic footage to industries that build our world.

Our Heritage

From a small cinematic team to a national leader in industrial UAV mapping.

2018: Inception

Founded as a boutique real estate aerial photography firm operating out of a small studio.

2021: Industrial Expansion

Acquired our first enterprise-grade RTK drones and transitioned into infrastructure mapping and 3D modeling.

2024: SaaS Dashboard Launch

Developed our proprietary client delivery portal, allowing enterprise clients to interact with point clouds securely.

2026: National Fleet

Operating a fleet of 50+ specialized UAVs across 12 regional offices with licensed Part 107 pilots.
